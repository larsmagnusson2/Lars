The homepage of Lars Magnusson

My name is Lars Magnusson. My background is in linguistics, and below you can click to read something I wrote about the grammaticality of spontaneous spoken Swedish some years ago. You can also see data from the research project I conducted during my three years at UNAM (the University of Namibia). The research topic was one of the Oshiwambo dialects of northern Namibia, Ochikwambi.
